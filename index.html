Providing Quality Health service since 1980

Welcome to midway hospital

Midway Hospital was established over four decades ago on the 6th of August 1980 to provide high standard, cost effective and reasonable healthcare services to improve the health status of the community.

We started as a clinic which was originally located at Abeka junction in a 2-bedroom rented house with a staff quarters. The clinic had a 7-bed capacity with the bathroom being used as the major Theatre and the kitchen as the Labour Ward.

In October 1988, we moved to our present location at Achimota with a 30-bed capacity and have since been expanding to provide quality healthcare.

Why Choose Us

we are committed to providing quality healthcare to our distinguished clients at affordable price.
